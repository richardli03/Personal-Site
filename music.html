Music

Whenever the icebreaker question "tell us one interesting fact about yourself" pops up, I automatically answer: "I write music." Everywhere I go, it becomes my "thing," the part of me I identify with most heavily. However, I'm looking to become a software engineer, which means there's no real reason for me to share this on my resume, even though it's such a huge part of my life. That's why it's here!

I've been writing music since I was 14. It's been a long journey since then. I started with plunking around in garageband on my iPad and putting them onto a YouTube channel and now I'm releasing songs I've fully mixed and mastered onto Apple Music and Spotify.
